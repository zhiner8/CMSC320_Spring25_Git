Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: We use Git to efficiently track changes, collaborate seamlessly with others, and maintain a reliable version history of our code.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science can be applied to healthcare, such as using machine learning to predict disease outbreaks based on patient data.

Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

Answer: Merging keeps the branch history, while rebasing moves your changes onto the latest version of the main branch.
